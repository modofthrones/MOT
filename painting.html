GoT Paintings Mod
This mod adds paintings inspired by the world of Westeros!

Now you can decorate your Minecraft home with Dragon scenr and house banner from the Game of Thrones universe



All paintings replace the default ones in-game, which means you can install this as a resource pack and use it on any server or realm.
